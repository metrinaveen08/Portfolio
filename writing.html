My Writing & Publications

As an extension of my interest in technology and human experience, I also craft compelling narratives. Here is a collection of my published works, from dark fantasy novels to existential short stories.

Echoes of Severed Dream

For centuries, the Five Civilizations have lived in a fragile peace, their powers drawn from one of two essential life forces: the Star Force or the Dark Force. You are born to one. You live by one.

But Shawn is an anomaly.

Born into the Human Civilization, he is surrounded by mysterious powers that defy the rules and unseen forces that want him controlled or dead. His very existence is a paradox that threatens to shatter the balance and plunge the universe back into war.

Hunted and afraid, Shawn must unravel the secret of his own origin. Is he a new kind of power, a bridge between the two... or a weapon that will be the end of them all?
